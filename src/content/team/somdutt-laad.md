bio: |
  Somdutt Laad is a seasoned Chartered Accountant and social development leader with nearly two decades of experience spanning the social sector, real estate, and finance. His unique blend of financial acumen, strategic thinking, and grassroots expertise has enabled him to drive impactful change at scale.
  He is currently serving as President & Trustee of Yuva Mitra, a Maharashtra based social development organization, focused on Water Resource Development, Livelihoods, Institutional building, Sustainable Agriculture, Health and Education. His leadership extends beyond the social sector, having spearheaded a family-owned construction business and worked as a Credit Underwriter at HDFC Ltd.
  Somdutt firmly believes that NGOs must scale up and replicate their initiatives to optimize resources, which can only be achieved through data-driven insights, technological support, and collaboration opportunities. This conviction drives Transunifyy's mission to leverage data, technology & thematic expertise for impactful social change.
  Somdutt holds a Chartered Accountant certification, a Master's in Marketing Management, and a Bachelor's in Commerce. His contributions to the sector have earned him the Excellence in Innovation Award, and he is certified in ESG Investing and Social Auditing.
